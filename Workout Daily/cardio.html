Cardio Workout :

Note :

  Take 30 - 45 Sec. break between each Workout

1.Jumping jacks with arm raises: ( 3x30sec )

  • Regular jumping jacks, but raise your arms overhead when you jump, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


2.Mountain Climbers: ( 3x30sec )

  • Start in a high plank position and bring your knees towards your chest one at a time, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


3.High Knees: ( 3x30sec )

  • Run in place while bringing your knees up high, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


4.Butt Kicks: ( 3x30sec )

  • Run in place while kicking your heels up towards your glutes, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


5.Lunges: ( 3x12 )

  • Step forward with one leg, lowering your hips until both knees are bent at 90 degrees, then push back to start. Repeat on the other side, 10 reps per leg.


6.Jumping squats: ( 3x12 )

  • Squat down as low as you can, then jump up explosively. Land softly and repeat, 3 sets of 10 reps.


7.Plank Jacks: ( 3x30sec )

  • Start in a high plank position and jump your feet out to the sides, then back together. Repeat, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


8.Burpees: ( 3x12 )

  • Squat down, place your hands on the floor, kick your legs back to a plank position, do a push-up (or modify to a knee push-up), jump your legs back in, and stand up straight. Repeat, 3 sets of 10 reps.


9.Stair steppers: ( 3x30sec )

  • Find a sturdy step or stair and step up and down continuously, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


10.Jumping lunges: ( 3x30sec )

  • Lunge forward and jump in the air, switching legs in mid-air. Land softly and repeat, 3 sets of 10 reps per leg.
