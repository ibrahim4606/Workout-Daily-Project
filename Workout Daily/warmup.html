Warm-up Workout :

Note :

  Take 30 - 45 Sec. break between each Workout

1.Jumping Jacks: ( 3x30sec )

  • Get your heart rate up and blood flowing with 3 sets of 30 seconds.


2.Arm Circles: ( 3x12 )

  • Forward and backward circles for 10 reps each arm to loosen up your shoulders.


3.High Knees: ( 3x30sec )

  • Run in place while bringing your knees up high, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


4.Butt Kicks: ( 3x30sec )

  • Run in place while kicking your heels up towards your glutes, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


5.Lunges: ( 3x12 )

  • Step forward with one leg, lowering your hips until both knees are bent at 90 degrees, then push back to start. Repeat on the other side, 10 reps per leg.


6.Squats: ( 3x12 )

  • Lower yourself down as if sitting in a chair, keeping your back straight and core engaged. Stand back up to start, 3 sets of 10 reps.


7.Plank: ( 3x30sec )

  • Get into a high plank position on your forearms and hold for 30 seconds, repeat 3 times.


8.Calf Raises ( 3x12 )

  • Rise up onto your toes and hold for a second, then lower back down. Repeat 15-20 times, 3 sets.


9.Neck Rolls: ( 3x12 )

  • Gentle circles in both directions, 10 reps each way, to ease neck tension.


10.Torso Twists: ( 3x30sec )

  • Rotate your upper body side-to-side, keeping your hips facing forward, 10 reps each side.
