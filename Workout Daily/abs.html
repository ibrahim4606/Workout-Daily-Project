ABS Workout :

Here's a killer ab workout you can do at home without any equipment! It includes 10 bodyweight exercises to target different areas of your abdominal muscles. Remember to modify or skip exercises as needed to maintain proper form and avoid injury.

Note :

  Take 30 - 45 Sec. break between each Workout

Workout

1.Crunches: ( 3x12 )

  • Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your fingertips behind your head, not interlaced.

  • Gently lift your upper body off the ground, squeezing your abs at the top.

  • Slowly lower back down to the starting position.


2.Reverse crunches: ( 3x12 )

  • Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands by your sides.

  • Gently lift your lower back and legs off the ground, bringing your knees towards your chest.

  • Slowly lower back down to the starting position.


3.Plank: ( 3x12 )

  • Start in a high plank position with your forearms on the floor and your body in a straight line from head to heels.

  • Engage your core and hold the position for as long as you can.


4.Side plank: ( 3x12 )

  • Start on your side with your elbow directly under your shoulder and your feet stacked.

  • Lift your hips off the ground so your body forms a straight line from head to heels.

  • Hold for as long as you can, then repeat on the other side.


5.Russian twists: ( 3x12 )

  • Sit on the floor with your knees bent and feet flat on the ground. Lean back slightly and lift your feet off the ground.

  • Rotate your torso to one side, then the other, without letting your lower back touch the ground.


6.Mountain Climbers: ( 3x30sec )

  • Start in a high plank position and bring your knees towards your chest one at a time, 3 sets of 30 seconds.


7.Dead Bugs: ( 3x12 )

  • Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor.

  • Extend one arm straight up towards the ceiling and the opposite leg straight out in front of you.

  • Lower your arm and leg down towards the floor at the same time, but don't let your lower back touch the ground.

  • Repeat with the other arm and leg.


8.Bird Dog: ( 3x12 )

  • Start on your hands and knees with your hands shoulder-width apart and your knees hip


9.Bicycle crunches: ( 3x12 )

  • Lie on your back with your knees bent and feet flat on the floor. Place your hands behind your head.

  • Bring one knee towards your chest while twisting your torso to bring the opposite elbow towards your knee.

  • Switch sides and repeat with the other leg.


10.Leg raises: ( 3x12 )

  • Lie on your back with your legs extended straight up towards the ceiling. Keep your feet together and toes pointed.

  • Slowly lower your legs down towards the floor until they are just above the ground.

  • Without using momentum, raise your legs back up to the starting position.
