Shoulder Workout :

Here's a bodyweight shoulder workout you can do at home without any equipment. It includes 10 exercises, each offering variations for different difficulty levels. Remember to modify or skip exercises as needed to maintain proper form and avoid injury.

Note :

  Take 30 - 45 Sec. break between each Workout

Workout

1.Regular Push-ups: ( 3x12 )

  • Beginner :  Do push-ups from your knees.

  • Intermediate :  Elevate your hands on a bench, chair, or sturdy object.

  • Advanced :  Do decline push-ups with your feet elevated on a bench or chair.


2.Diamond Push-ups: ( 3x12 )

  • Beginner :  Keep your hands wider than shoulder-width apart.

  • Intermediate :  Bring your hands closer together, forming a diamond shape with your thumbs and forefingers.

  • Advanced :  Do decline diamond push-ups with your feet elevated.


3.Pike Push-ups: ( 3x12 )

  • Place your hands shoulder-width apart on the floor in front of you, keeping your body in a straight line from head to heels.Walk your feet forward until your body forms an inverted V shape, with your hands close to your shoulders and your hips high.

  • Lower your chest towards the ground, keeping your elbows close to your body.Push back up to the starting position.

  • Beginner :  Do this exercise with your knees bent.

  • Intermediate :  Keep your legs straight.

  • Advanced :  Do handstand push-ups against a wall.


4.Front Raises: ( 3x12 )

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and core engaged. Raise your arms straight out in front of you, palms facing down, until they are parallel to the ground.

  • Lower your arms back down to your sides.

  • Beginner :  Do this exercise with your arms bent at a 90-degree angle.

  • Intermediate :  Hold water bottles or books in your hands for added weight.

  • Advanced :  Try single-arm raises.


5.Lateral Raises: ( 3x12 )

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and core engaged. Raise your arms out to the sides, palms facing down, until they are parallel to the ground.

  • Lower your arms back down to your sides.

  • Beginner :  Do this exercise with your arms bent at a 90-degree angle.

  • Intermediate :  Hold water bottles or books in your hands for added weight.

  • Advanced :  Try single-arm raises.


6.Bird Dog: ( 3x12 )

  • Start on your hands and knees with your hands shoulder-width apart and your knees hip


7.Military Press ( 3x12 )

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and core engaged. Raise your arms overhead, palms facing forward, until your elbows are straight.

  • Lower your arms back down to your shoulders.

  • Beginner :  Do this exercise with your arms bent at a 90-degree angle.

  • Intermediate :  Hold water bottles or books in your hands for added weight.

  • Advanced :   Try single-arm presses.
