Triceps Workout :

Here's a killer triceps workout you can do at home without any equipment! It includes 10 bodyweight exercises, each offering variations for different difficulty levels. Remember to modify or skip exercises as needed to maintain proper form and avoid injury.

Note :

  Take 30 - 45 Sec. break between each Workout

Workout

1.Tricep Dips : ( 3x12 )

  • Sit on the edge of a sturdy chair or bench with your hands shoulder-width apart on the edge.Slide your body off the chair so your arms are straight and your feet are flat on the floor.

  • Lower your body down until your elbows are bent at a 90-degree angle.Push back up to the starting position.

  • Beginner :  Bend your knees for more support.

  • Intermediate :  Keep your legs straight out in front of you.

  • Advanced :  Elevate your feet on another chair or bench for a greater challenge.


2.Diamond Push-ups: ( 3x12 )

  • Beginner :  Keep your hands wider than shoulder-width apart.

  • Intermediate :  Bring your hands closer together, forming a diamond shape with your thumbs and forefingers.

  • Advanced :  Do decline diamond push-ups with your feet elevated.


3.Chair Tricep Extensions: ( 3x12 )

  • Sit on a sturdy chair with your feet flat on the floor and hands behind your head, fingers interlaced.Press your elbows back and straighten your arms, pushing your hands into your head.

  • Slowly lower your hands back down to your head.

  • Beginner :  Start with fewer reps or use lighter resistance (water bottle, book).

  • Intermediate :  Increase the number of reps or try single-arm extensions.

  • Advanced :  Add weight by holding heavier objects in your hands.


4.Close-grip Push-ups ( 3x12 )

  • Get into a push-up position with your hands closer together than shoulder-width apart.Lower your chest towards the ground and then push back up.

  • This exercise also works your chest and shoulders.

  • Beginner :  Do push-ups from your knees.

  • Intermediate :  Elevate your hands on a bench or chair.

  • Advanced :  Do decline push-ups with your feet elevated.


5.Tricep Push-ups: ( 3x12 )

  • Get into a push-up position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your fingers pointing inwards.Lower your chest towards the ground and then push back up.

  • This exercise also works your chest and shoulders.

  • Beginner :  Do push-ups from your knees.

  • Intermediate :  Elevate your hands on a bench or chair.

  • Advanced :  Do decline push-ups with your feet elevated.
